About JOH Technologies

Your trusted partner for premium technology solutions in Mbarara, Uganda since 2017. We provide high-quality computers, accessories, and expert IT services to individuals and businesses.

Our Story

From a small tech shop to Mbarara's leading IT solutions provider

Our Beginnings

Founded in 2017, JOH Technologies started as a small computer repair shop on High Street, Mbarara. Our passion for technology and commitment to customer satisfaction quickly helped us grow into a full-service IT company.

Today, we operate from two convenient locations in Mbarara, serving both walk-in customers and corporate clients across Western Uganda.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, reliable, and high-quality technology solutions that empower individuals and businesses in Uganda to achieve their goals through innovative technology.

We believe that everyone deserves access to quality tech products and expert support, regardless of their technical knowledge or budget.

Meet Our Founder

With over 7 years of experience in the IT industry, our founder has built JOH Technologies on the principles of integrity, quality, and exceptional customer service.

"At JOH Technologies, we don't just sell computers - we provide solutions. Our team is passionate about helping our customers find the right technology for their needs and ensuring they get the most out of their investment."
